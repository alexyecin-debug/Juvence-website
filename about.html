About

Built by a business owner,
for business owners.

I own a restaurant and a wedding hall — Sandra Palace. For years, I dealt with the same problems every business owner knows: missed calls during peak hours, a website that looked like it was built in 2012, and a Google listing that was half-wrong. I tried hiring agencies. They'd take the money, build something mediocre, and disappear. Sound familiar?

So I started fixing it myself. I tested AI phone agents on my own businesses first. I rebuilt my websites. I optimized my Google profiles until they actually brought in new customers. I set up automated booking and SMS reminders. And it worked — not in theory, but in practice. Fewer missed calls. More bookings. Real, measurable growth.

That's when I realized: if these tools work this well for my businesses, they'd work for every local business. The salon down the street. The dental clinic. The barbershop. The HVAC company. They all have the same problems — they just don't have time to solve them. That's why I started Juvence. Not as another agency that sells and disappears, but as a digital team that stays, optimizes, and helps you grow.
