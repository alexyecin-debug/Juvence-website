Services

What we do

Five services. All connected. All managed for you.

01

AI Phone Agent

Every missed call is a missed customer. Your AI phone agent answers every call, 24/7, in French and English. It sounds human, books appointments, sends SMS confirmations, and routes urgent calls to you. Your customers never hit voicemail again.

How it works

1

We learn your business — services, hours, common questions, booking rules.

2

We configure and train your AI agent with your specific scripts and tone.

3

We connect it to your phone line. Calls are answered instantly, appointments booked automatically.

What's included

  • 24/7 AI call answering in French and English
  • Appointment booking with calendar sync
  • SMS confirmations to customers
  • Urgent call routing to your phone
  • Monthly call analytics and optimization
  • Custom greeting and scripts
Average clients see 40% fewer missed calls in the first month.

02

Website

Your website is your storefront. If it's slow, ugly, or missing on mobile, you're losing customers before they even call. We build clean, fast, mobile-first websites designed specifically for local businesses. No templates. No monthly platform fees. Just a site that works.

How it works

1

We audit your current site (or lack of one) and map out what your customers need to see.

2

We design and build a custom site tailored to your business and brand.

3

We launch, connect analytics, and optimize monthly based on real visitor data.

What's included

  • Custom design — no templates
  • Mobile-first, fast-loading pages
  • SEO fundamentals built in
  • Contact forms and click-to-call
  • Google Analytics integration
  • Monthly performance optimization
Clients typically see 2-3x more website inquiries within 60 days of launch.

03

Google Business Profile

When someone searches "restaurant near me" or "barber Laval," Google decides who shows up first. An optimized Google Business Profile is the difference between being found and being invisible. We make sure your listing is complete, accurate, and working for you.

How it works

1

We claim or audit your existing Google profile and identify gaps.

2

We optimize every field — photos, services, hours, categories, description, and Q&A.

3

We manage reviews, post updates, and track how customers are finding you.

What's included

  • Full profile setup and optimization
  • Professional photo guidelines
  • Review management and response templates
  • Weekly Google Posts
  • Monthly search performance reports
  • Competitor visibility tracking
Optimized profiles see an average 70% increase in direction requests and calls from Google.

04

Booking System

Phone tag kills bookings. Customers want to book when it's convenient for them — at 10pm on a Sunday, during their lunch break, between meetings. An online booking system lets them do that while keeping your calendar organized and conflict-free.

How it works

1

We map your services, durations, availability, and booking rules.

2

We set up a booking page connected to your calendar with automatic confirmations.

3

Customers book online, you see it instantly. No overlaps, no confusion.

What's included

  • Online booking page with your branding
  • Calendar sync (Google, Apple, Outlook)
  • Automatic email and SMS confirmations
  • Appointment reminders to reduce no-shows
  • Buffer time and capacity management
  • Integration with your website and Google profile
Businesses with online booking see up to 30% fewer no-shows with automated reminders.

05

SMS Campaigns

Email gets ignored. Phone calls interrupt. SMS gets read — 98% open rate within 3 minutes. We set up automated text campaigns that remind customers about appointments, request reviews after visits, and re-engage clients who haven't been back in a while.

How it works

1

We define your campaign goals — reminders, reviews, re-engagement, or promotions.

2

We write the messages, set the triggers, and configure the automation.

3

Texts go out automatically. You see results in your monthly report.

What's included

  • Appointment reminder sequences
  • Post-visit review request automation
  • Re-engagement campaigns for inactive clients
  • Promotional campaign setup
  • Compliance and opt-out management
  • Monthly performance reporting
Review request texts typically generate 5-10 new Google reviews per month.
